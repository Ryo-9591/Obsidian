# UNION
## NULLを使って無理やり列数と型を合わせる例
`SELECT employee_id, department_id`
`FROM employees`
`WHERE department_id = 10`

`UNION`

`SELECT department_id, CAST(NULL AS NUMBER)`
`FROM departments`
`WHERE location_id = 1700;`
→比較する前に明示的にNUMBER型にキャストをしている
